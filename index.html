The problem

Manual data work is a liability,
not just an inefficiency

When analytics depend on one person, one process, or one undocumented workflow, the risk isn't just wasted hours — it's audit exposure, compliance gaps, and fragile infrastructure that leaves when people do.

Hours lost to manual work

Teams spend 30–60% of their time moving data between systems, reformatting reports, and running the same checks every week.

Single points of failure

When only one person understands how a workflow runs, every vacation, departure, or reorg becomes a risk event.

Audit and governance exposure

Tools only experts can use

Data infrastructure built by specialists, for specialists — leaving business teams unable to self-serve without submitting a ticket.
